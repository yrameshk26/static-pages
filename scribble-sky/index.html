Frequently asked questions

Does Scribble Sky need the internet?

No. The app works fully offline. There are no accounts and no sign-in.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

No ads and no in-app purchases. Scribble Sky is a one-time purchase — pay once and all future updates are free.

How do I save my child's artwork?

Behind the parent gate, tap Save. With your permission, the app adds the finished picture to your device's photo gallery. This happens only on your device — nothing is uploaded.

How do I remove profiles or saved pictures?

Open the in-app parent settings to clear profiles, gallery, and progress, or simply uninstall the app to remove everything.

Which devices are supported?

iPad and iPhone running a current version of iOS/iPadOS. The app is designed for young children (ages 2–5) and their families.

Privacy Policy — in short

Last updated: 25 June 2026

Scribble Sky collects no data about you or your child. It works fully offline, has no ads, no accounts, no sign-in, and no tracking. Everything your child makes stays on your device.

Who this is for

Scribble Sky is an offline, ad-free drawing and coloring app made for young children (ages 2–5) and their families. We take children's privacy seriously and have designed the app to collect no personal information.

Information we collect

None. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not ask for names, emails, phone numbers, locations, or any account.

Things the app saves are kept only on your device and are never sent anywhere:

  • Kid profiles (an emoji avatar and an on-device nickname you choose)
  • Saved artwork and gallery
  • Game progress and stars

You can remove all of this at any time from the in-app parent settings, or by uninstalling the app.

No internet, no ads, no analytics

  • The app makes no network connections and the Android build ships without the INTERNET permission.
  • There are no advertisements and no in-app purchases.
  • There is no analytics and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Permissions

  • Photos / Media (optional): only used so a grown-up can save your child's finished artwork to the device's photo gallery. This is behind a parental gate, happens entirely on your device, and is never uploaded.
  • Vibration: for gentle haptic feedback while drawing.

Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

Because no personal information is collected, transmitted, or shared, the app does not knowingly gather data from children. We do not build profiles, show interest-based content, or contact users.

Data sharing

We share nothing with anyone, because we collect nothing.
